Office Virtual Tour

Welcome to Kaspersky’s Virtual Office Tour, an inside look at where we dissect the most complex malware in the world and develop the latest cybersecurity technologies. Join the 360° virtual tour with our furry, green friend, Midori Kuma, who will guide you through our office and introduce you to our teams.

Office
360º VR

Our head office in Moscow is more than just a building. It is an experience. You’ll be treated to a museum on the ground floor, showcasing some impressive artwork and artifacts that offer a glimpse into the history of Kaspersky. So, step inside and let Midori show you around. You can even wear your virtual reality headset, if you have one, for a more immersive 3D tour.

Salvador Dali sculpted the piece in bronze, and its shade of emerald green nearly matches Kaspersky’s brand colors. Quite fitting, don’t you think?

Entrance

As you enter, take a look to your left. The big green elephant with insect-like legs looks like a Dali sculpture: and it is! We told you this was also a virtual museum tour too, didn’t we? The creation is one of several Triumphant Elephants by Salvador Dali, and Eugene Kaspersky purchased it at a London auction in 2014. Legend has it that someone dared him to buy an elephant — and he had the last laugh.

You will also find a Formula 1 steering wheel gifted to Kaspersky by Marco Mattiacci, the former CEO of Ferrari. This celebrates the long and fruitful relationship that Kaspersky and the Ferrari F1 team have enjoyed over the years.

Entrance

Continue walking through the museum, then turn right to find our very first commercial product in its original packaging, as well as some of the awards Kaspersky has received over the years. Our products take part in almost a hundred independent tests every year. In most cases they secure first place, and make it into the top three almost 80% of the time.

Servers

But enough history for now! Follow Midori Kuma towards the lift to take a look at the work we do today. On Level 0 you will see examples of Kaspersky servers that are located around the world.

For example, servers for our main cloud system are located in Germany, Canada, and China, to name just a few, and Kaspersky data processing servers can be found in Switzerland.

RnD Center

On the next floor, you’ll find our Research and Development Centre, where Kaspersky teams work hard every day to keep our customers safe.

The antivirus lab is the heart of the company, where automated systems analyze and process two million files every day, and where human experts pick up the most complex threats for further analysis. We warmly invite you to meet our hardworking teams and learn about what they do. These include our Threat Research Department and our Targeted Attacks Research Group, which deal with the most sophisticated cybersecurity attacks.

Don’t forget to visit our Global Research & Analysis Team (GReAT) — these guys are stars when it comes to advanced persistent threat research.

RnD Center

There are more systems and teams to explore before ending your virtual tour in Switzerland, the location for Kaspersky transparency centers and where the data of our European customers is processed.

Switzerland

Visit the top floor to admire the beautiful, scenic view of the Swiss Alps and meet a very special person who can explain some of Kaspersky’s transparency efforts.
